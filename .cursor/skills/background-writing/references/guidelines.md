# Background / Related Work Writing Guidelines

- **Source**: Use only content from **literature-notes** (grouped by theme). Do not add claims beyond what the notes support.
- **Background**: Provide essential technical context and notation; cite papers by theme; keep concise.
- **Related work**: Position the project relative to prior art; clarify similarities and differences; one short paragraph per theme or method family.
- **Tone**: Neutral, factual. Avoid unsupported superlatives; if a note says "main result X", you may say "X" but not "dramatically improves" unless the note supports it.
- **Output**: Bullets or short paragraphs suitable for pasting into `latex/sections/2-background.tex` or `latex/sections/6-related-work.tex`; mark where citations go.
