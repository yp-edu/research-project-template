# Section Goals (High Level)

- **Introduction**: Motivate the problem, state the research question, highlight gaps, and summarize contributions clearly and concretely.
- **Background**: Provide essential technical context and notation needed to understand the method and experiments.
- **Methods**: Describe the proposed approach precisely enough to re-implement, including any key design choices and their intuition.
- **Experiments**: Describe experimental setup, datasets, metrics, and baselines; present results fairly and transparently.
- **Discussion**: Interpret results, discuss strengths and limitations, and suggest future directions.
- **Related Work**: Position the work relative to prior art, clarifying similarities and differences.
- **Conclusion**: Recap the main findings and contributions without making unsupported claims.
