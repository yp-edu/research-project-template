# Ideation Checklist

When evaluating research ideas during the ideation phase, ensure you address:

1. **Feasibility**: Can this be done with available data, compute, and timeline? What are the main blockers?
2. **Impact**: Who is the audience? What would change if this works? Is the gap from prior work clear and meaningful?
3. **Statistical significance**: How will you show the effect is real? What sample size, metrics, and comparisons are needed? What would a null result look like?
4. **Risks**: What could invalidate the idea? What assumptions are you making?
