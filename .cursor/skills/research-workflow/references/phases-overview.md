# Research Phases Overview

- **Project setup**: Establish project metadata, repository structure, and basic tooling.
- **Ideation & literature**: Clarify the research question, explore prior work, and identify gaps.
- **Baseline development**: Implement and validate simple and stronger baselines with clear metrics.
- **Contribution development**: Design and implement your novel method, including key ablations.
- **Review & revision**: Improve clarity, rigor, and reproducibility; prepare for submission or internal milestones.
