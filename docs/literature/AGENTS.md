# Literature Guidance

- Literature notes capture facts that matter to this project: citation or link, take-home message, relevant method, baseline, dataset, limitation, claim, and project relevance.
- Connect papers to questions, experiments, claims, decisions, and paper sections when useful.
- Move synthesized background and related-work prose into `latex/` as the argument stabilizes.
- Name literature notes `YYYY-MM-DD-short-title.md` using the date the paper is recorded.
- Keep category-level summaries in `README.md` factual; leave it title-only until there is something real to summarize.
- Track literature notes from the category dashboard as `[paper](YYYY-MM-DD-paper-title.md): Pending`, then replace `Pending` with the takeaway, relevant claim, or decision it supports.
