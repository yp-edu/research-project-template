# Conference Requirements Reference

Quick reference for major ML/AI conference submission requirements.

## NeurIPS (Neural Information Processing Systems)

- **Abstract:** 150-250 words
- **Page Limit:** 9 pages (main text) + unlimited references + unlimited appendices
- **Format:** NeurIPS LaTeX style
- **Double-blind:** Yes
- **Code/Data:** Encouraged but not required
- **Deadline:** Usually late May/early June
- **Notification:** Late September
- **Camera-ready:** Late October

## ICML (International Conference on Machine Learning)

- **Abstract:** 200 words
- **Page Limit:** 8 pages (main text) + unlimited references + unlimited appendices
- **Format:** ICML LaTeX style
- **Double-blind:** Yes
- **Code/Data:** Encouraged but not required
- **Deadline:** Usually late January/early February
- **Notification:** Late April
- **Camera-ready:** Late May

## ICLR (International Conference on Learning Representations)

- **Abstract:** 200 words
- **Page Limit:** 8 pages (main text) + unlimited references + unlimited appendices
- **Format:** ICLR LaTeX style
- **Double-blind:** Yes
- **Code/Data:** Strongly encouraged (OpenReview integration)
- **Deadline:** Usually late September/early October
- **Notification:** Late January
- **Camera-ready:** Late February

## AAAI (Association for the Advancement of Artificial Intelligence)

- **Abstract:** 150-200 words
- **Page Limit:** 7 pages (main text) + 2 pages references
- **Format:** AAAI LaTeX style
- **Double-blind:** Yes
- **Code/Data:** Encouraged
- **Deadline:** Usually late August/early September
- **Notification:** Late November
- **Camera-ready:** Late December

## Common Requirements

### Reproducibility
- **Code:** Most conferences encourage code release
- **Data:** Public datasets preferred, private data requires access instructions
- **Hyperparameters:** Should be clearly specified
- **Random seeds:** Should be fixed and reported
- **Compute resources:** Should be documented

### Ethics
- **Broader Impact Statement:** Required by NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR
- **Ethics Review:** May be required for sensitive applications
- **Data Ethics:** Must address data collection, consent, privacy

### Submission Process
- **Abstract submission:** Usually required before full submission
- **Rebuttal period:** Most conferences allow author response
- **Anonymization:** Must remove author names, affiliations, acknowledgments

### Review Criteria
- **Novelty:** Is the contribution novel?
- **Significance:** Does it advance the field?
- **Technical quality:** Is the method sound?
- **Experimental validation:** Are experiments thorough?
- **Clarity:** Is the paper well-written?
- **Reproducibility:** Can results be reproduced?
