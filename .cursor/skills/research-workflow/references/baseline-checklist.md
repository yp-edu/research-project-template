# Baseline Development Checklist

Before or during baseline development, ensure you:

1. Clarify the research question and success metrics.
2. Identify existing baselines (in the literature or current code).
3. Implement at least one simple, reliable baseline.
4. Design 1–3 stronger baselines that are feasible but informative.
5. Log metrics, configs, and seeds for reproducibility.
6. Plan how baselines will appear in the paper (tables, figures, or text).
