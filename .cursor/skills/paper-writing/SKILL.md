# Paper Writing Skill

## When to Use

- The user is drafting or revising sections under `latex/sections/`.
- You need reminders of each section’s role and structure.
- You want lightweight templates for turning results and notes into prose.

## Instructions

- Use `references/section-goals.md` for high-level goals of each standard section.
- Use any additional outlines or snippets stored in this skill’s references or assets.

When helping with writing:

1. Ask which sections to focus on and what constraints apply (venue, length, style).
2. Briefly restate the goal of the section using `section-goals.md`.
3. Propose an outline or revision plan before editing.
4. Make small, reviewable edits that stay faithful to the underlying code, experiments, and literature.
5. Summarize what changed and what evidence (files/results) supports new or updated claims.
